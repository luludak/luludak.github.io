About Me

My name is Nikolaos (Nick) and I am an AI Testing Researcher at the University of Edinburgh.
Recently, I obtained my Ph.D. in Informatics from the same institution. Previously, I was employed at Oracle Labs, Workable, Accenture and the Institute of Computer Science - FORTH, actively working on numerous projects, using state-of-the-art technologies and principles, both as researcher and software engineer. I am also a co-founder at Carbon Glance, a startup building a SaaS related to carbon regulations.
In addition to my Ph.D. studies, I hold an M.Sc. and a B.Sc. from the Computer Science Department of the University of Crete, focused on the fields of Software Engineering, Information Systems and Human-Computer Interaction. For me, studying Computer Science and working in this fascinating field is a dream fulfilled and that I am happy to constantly learn more and work with enthusiasm and passion and produce excellent and impactful results.
